Support Victims Affected by the Turkey-Syria Earthquake

The recent earthquake in Turkey and Syria continues to impact millions of people in the area that were left without shelter and basic supplies.


What can we do?

Donations can be sent to:

  • Turkey and Syria Earthquake Relief Fund by GlobalGiving
  • Syrian American Medical Society
  • UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency
  • The United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund (UNICEF)
